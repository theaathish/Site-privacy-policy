Request to Delete Your Account and Data

This page is for users of the Track My Leave app who wish to permanently delete their account and associated data stored in our system, including Firebase.

How to Request Deletion

Please send an email to gokulmail015@gmail.com with the subject line:

"Delete My Track My Leave Account"

Make sure to include:

  • The email address associated with your account
  • Your full name (optional)
  • Any other identifying info (if applicable)

What Happens Next

  • Your account data stored in Firebase (e.g., name, email, leave requests) will be deleted permanently.
  • Deletion will be processed within 7 days of receiving your request.
  • You will receive a confirmation once the deletion is complete.

Retention

No data will be retained after your account is deleted. However, anonymous usage data (if any) will remain for analytics purposes only and cannot be linked back to you.

If you have any questions, please contact us at gokulmail015@gmail.com.
